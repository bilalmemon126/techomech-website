About Us

Welcome to Techomech

We specialize in manufacturing, installation, maintenance, and complete overhauling of mechanical and electronic systems. Our expertise includes pipe and metal fabrication, CNC machining, structural welding, and erection services.

As a reliable supplier of electrical and electronic parts, we ensure quality and efficiency in every project. Additionally, we provide top-notch CCTV camera solutions, from supply to installation, enhancing security for businesses and industries.

Our Services

Manufacturing & Maintenance

We specialize in manufacturing high-quality industrial parts and providing installation, maintenance, and repair services. Our team ensures efficient overhauling and renovation of machinery and equipment, helping businesses maintain peak operational performance.

Pipe Fabrication & Welding

We offer expert pipe fabrication, welding, erection, and insulation services for industrial applications. Our precision-engineered solutions ensure durability, reliability, and efficiency in fluid and gas transport systems.

Metal Fabrication & CNC Machining

Our advanced CNC machining and auto milling services provide custom metal fabrication solutions. We create high-precision components tailored to meet industrial requirements, ensuring quality and accuracy in every project.

Structural Fabrication & Erection

We design, fabricate, and erect structural steel components for various industrial and commercial applications. Our expertise in welding and installation ensures strong and secure frameworks for buildings, plants, and other infrastructure.

Electrical & Electronic Supplies

As a trusted supplier, we provide high-quality electrical and electronic parts, including control panels, circuit breakers, transformers, and more. Our reliable products support various industries, ensuring smooth and safe operations.

CCTV Camera Solutions

We offer a complete range of CCTV cameras and surveillance systems, from supply to professional installation. Our security solutions help businesses and industries enhance safety, monitor operations, and protect assets effectively.
